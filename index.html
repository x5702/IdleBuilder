Battle

Turn based

  1. Recon phase, winner get initiate
  2. Air attack phase, winner get air attack bonus
  3. Submarine attack phase
  4. Long range attack phase
  5. Short range attack phase
  6. Torpedo attack phase
  7. ...?

Defend

  • All territories need defend force
  • Fail to defend -> production effeciency down
  • You'll never lose your territory

Fleet and Ship Designer

  • Preset Equipments and Ships in your fleet
  • Battle starts automatically when everything is ready
  • Send reinforcements to defence teams automatically

Enemies

  • Procedually upgrade equipments and have larger ships
  • You get their technology by collection wreckage
